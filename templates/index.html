Iris Species Classification

Iris is a family of flowers containing several species such as the iris.versicolor, iris.setosa, and iris.virginica. The differnt species can be classified considering length and width of petals and sepals. This site will help you to identify the correct species. The only thing you need to do is to enter length and width of petals and sepals.
